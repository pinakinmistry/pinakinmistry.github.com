Full Stack Developers - Wave of Change

 

Full Stack Developers in place of conventional (technology/tier layered) developers is a wave of change happening in IT industry. This change has come to us because we live in age of Facebook and Twitter where an individual or a small bunch of individuals hand crafted small ideas into very attractive networking sites in matter of few weeks. And in very few years and with very small workforce, they have turned these small ideas to multi billion dollar social networking sites with millions of users/followers. These individuals are full stack developers doing things top to bottom, end to end with only sky as the limit. They inspire us to embrace this wave of change.

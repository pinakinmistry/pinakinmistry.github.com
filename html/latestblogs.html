Why Blog?

 

If you think why to blog, here is the answer. Blog is a modern mode of communication to 'Express Yourself'. Since it is on web, the audience could be anyone with computer and internet access, making it much more powerful than email or any other mode available to us. We can blog about our innovative ideas, researches and creations and share it with entire world.

So why wait, just express yourself!
